Academic Excellence

Our rigorous curriculum challenges students to think critically, solve problems creatively, and develop a lifelong love of learning.

Our Academic Approach

At Funmi Schools, we offer a comprehensive academic program that balances core subjects with specialized electives, preparing students for success in college and beyond. Our curriculum emphasizes critical thinking, collaboration, and real-world application of knowledge.

With small class sizes and dedicated faculty, we provide personalized attention to help each student reach their full potential.

Graduation Requirements

To earn a Funmi Schools diploma, students must complete the following credits across four years:

Subject Area Credits Required
English 4
Mathematics 3
Science 3
Social Studies 3
World Language 2
Physical Education 1
Arts 1
Electives 5
Total Credits 22

Additionally, students must complete 40 hours of community service and a senior capstone project to graduate.
