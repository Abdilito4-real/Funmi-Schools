Our Story, Mission, and Values

For over three decades, Funmi Schools has been shaping young minds and preparing leaders of tomorrow.

Our History

Founded in 1985 with just 200 students, Funmi Schools has grown into one of the region's most respected educational institutions, serving over 1,200 students today.

1985

School Founding

Funmi Schools opens its doors with 200 students and 15 faculty members in a small building in downtown Kaduna.

1995

New Campus Construction

We move to our current 25-acre campus with state-of-the-art facilities, allowing us to expand our academic and extracurricular offerings.

2005

IB Program Accreditation

Funmi becomes the first school in the region to offer the International Baccalaureate Diploma Programme.

2020

STEM Center Opening

Our new $5 million STEM Center opens, featuring advanced laboratories, maker spaces, and technology classrooms.

Our Mission & Values

At Funmi Schools, we are committed to providing a transformative educational experience that prepares students for success in college, career, and life.

Academic Excellence

We maintain high academic standards while fostering intellectual curiosity and a lifelong love of learning.

Character Development

We emphasize integrity, respect, and responsibility to develop students of strong character.

Inclusive Community

We celebrate diversity and create a welcoming environment where all students feel valued.

Innovation

We embrace new teaching methods and technologies to prepare students for a changing world.

Global Perspective

We develop students who understand global issues and can collaborate across cultures.

Service

We instill a commitment to serving others and making positive contributions to society.
